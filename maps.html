Maps



The Senvala Map

Created by the adventurer Senvala and dragon Rosmir, this map provides a dragons-eye view of the civilized world. Widely considered to be the most geographically accurate map, its utility does suffer from being several decades out of date.

Marnos Travel Guide

Created by the merchant guildmaster Marnos of Brelen. This map was created to aid guests at the Brelen Caravansary calculate the length of their travels. While the distance of road between the settlements is accurate for calculating travel, the other areas are not to scale. This makes it unreliable to use for off-road travel.
